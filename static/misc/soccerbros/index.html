About SoccerBros

Fun, fast-paced 1 on 1 soccer game with lots of action! Pick from a variety of characters and let the play begin. Go for crazy shots, make that penalty kick, or maybe even tackle your opponent!

The controls are very simple: Use either the arrow keys or wasd to control your bro. Space bar will shoot if you have the ball and tackle if you are on defense.

Be sure to allow notifications so you will know when fun new soccer characters are added to the game!

FAQ

Why isn't the game loading?

You should check your internet connection and make sure our site isn't blocked. Also, disable all ad blockers.

How do I play?

Use your arrow keys and put the ball into the net!

Which browser should I use?

You should use chrome. It is the only browser guaranteed to work. Soccer Bros has also been tested to work on FireFox and Safari, but not guaranteed.
